Look on my works, ye Mighty, and despair!

Hello! I am Yuanshen Lin, a Ph.D. student in the School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Lei Zhang. I obtained my bachelor's degree from the Wu Wenjun Honors Program, School of Mathematical Sciences, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I was advised by Prof. Zhenli Xu on research in computational mathematics. My academic interests lie broadly in computational mathematics, machine learning, and their real-world applications. Currently, my research focuses on model reduction, fast algorithms, and the construction of solution landscapes.

In my free time, I like to listen to music, which helps me stay focused and creative.

Feel free to check out my work and get in touch!
